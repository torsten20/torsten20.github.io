Getting around

The Netherlands is renowned for its excellent cycling infrastructure. If you’d like to take advantage of this during the hackathon, bikes are available for rent directly at the WICC Hotel.

Alternatively, public transport is also an option. Bus schedules and fare information are available online at 9292.nl, and tickets can be purchased on the bus.

WICC address: Lawickse Allee 9, 6701 AN Wageningen WUR Campus - Forum building: Droevendaalsesteeg 2, 6708 PB Wageningen.
